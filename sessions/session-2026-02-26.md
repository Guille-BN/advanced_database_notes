## What I understood
- An aggregate can take the average, sum, count, etc. of a whole column (its operant).
- Group by (column), takes all the rows with the same value for the given column, and makes them tables in and of themselves to operate into. e.g. SELECT category, avg(price) FROM productos GROUP BY category.
